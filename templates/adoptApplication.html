The regular adoption fee is $70 for each cat. All cats over 10 years old have a reduced adoption fee of $40. These fees include tax. The fee includes vaccinations and being neutered/spayed.

Once the form has been filled out, someone will get back to you within a few days. When you are invited in for a meet and greet, you will be asked to fill out some additional questions. Please ensure you bring at least two pieces of ID (one must be government issued such as a driver's license or passport). Please bring the name of your vet so we can fax over your cat's health records as well as provide you with a copy. The fee can be paid by cash or cheque. Be sure to bring a cat carrier and a soft blanket or towel in preparation to bring your new family member home.
